AIUS marketplace


How does it work
  • Miners can deposit rewards in ETH or ERC-20 tokens to incentivize AIUS holders to deposit to specific validators.
  • AIUS holders looking to swap to ETH or ERC-20 tokens may deposit directly to incentivized validators to earn rewards.

Each bribe has a rate (per 1 AIUS) set by the depositor.

Please note: You must deposit through this dapp/contract to claim rewards. There are no retroactive rewards.
